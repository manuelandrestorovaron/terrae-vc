Investment Thesis

The built environment is the next
frontier of deep innovation.

The built environment is one of the largest contributors to carbon emissions and one of the least digitized sectors in the global economy. That gap is where the most important entrepreneurial opportunities of the next decade will emerge. We back founders who are reimagining how the built environment is created, operated, and sustained.

01

Urgent Climate Pressure

Construction and infrastructure account for nearly 40% of global CO2 emissions. Regulation, capital markets, and customer demand are converging to force rapid decarbonization across the value chain.

02

Significant Modernization Gap

Construction remains one of the least digitized industries globally. Process inefficiency, fragmented data, and manual operations create structural opportunities for technology-enabled disruption.

03

Operator Edge

Our edge comes from operator experience. We have led businesses in construction materials, digital transformation, and sustainability across 50+ countries. We understand the industry from the inside — its decision cycles, its constraints, and where technology can genuinely unlock value.

Investment Criteria

What we look for
in a founder.

01

Early Traction

Functional prototypes or operational pilots. Exploratory agreements or letters of intent. Evidence that the idea has been tested against reality, not just designed on paper.

02

Domain-Deep Founders

Passion, experience, and transformative vision. Founders who understand the industry they are disrupting from the inside — not just the technology, but the customer, the market structure, and the decision cycle.

03

Strategic Clarity

A compelling narrative backed by technical and operational rigor. The ability to articulate what problem is being solved, for whom, and why this team is uniquely positioned to solve it.

04

Scalable Business Model

Business models with regional scalability and global vocation. We look for ventures where the unit economics improve with scale and where the path from pre-seed to Series A is legible.
