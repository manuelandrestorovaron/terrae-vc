About Terrae

Two platforms.
One purpose.

The business environment is being reshaped by three converging forces: the pressure to grow sustainably, the rise of the empowered customer, and the acceleration of digital technology. Companies that treat these as separate agendas fall behind. Those that integrate them gain a compounding advantage.

At the same time, the built environment — one of the largest contributors to carbon emissions and one of the least digitized sectors globally — is entering a decade of foundational disruption. Terrae Capital invests at that frontier.

The Founder

Manuel Toro

Founder · Terrae Partners & Terrae Capital

Manuel Toro brings over 21 years of executive experience at a global leader in construction materials, leading the integration of sustainability, customer excellence, and digital transformation across 50+ countries and 2,500+ people.

He led a sustainable product and solutions portfolio to $7.6B in revenue, achieving a Net Promoter Score of 74 against an industry average of 40. His academic formation includes graduate programs at MIT, and executive credentials from Stanford, INSEAD, IE Madrid, and LSE.
